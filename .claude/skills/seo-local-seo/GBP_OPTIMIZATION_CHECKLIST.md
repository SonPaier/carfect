# Google Business Profile Optimization Checklist

Field-by-field checklist for optimizing your Google Business Profile.

---

## Core Information

- [ ] **Business name** — Exact legal name, no extra keywords
- [ ] **Primary category** — Most specific accurate category
- [ ] **Additional categories** — All relevant secondary categories (up to 9)
- [ ] **Address** — Complete, accurate, matches website and citations
- [ ] **Service area** — Defined if service-area business (no address shown)
- [ ] **Phone number** — Local number with area code (not toll-free)
- [ ] **Website** — Links to location-specific page (not just homepage)
- [ ] **Hours** — Accurate for all days, including special hours
- [ ] **Holiday hours** — Set for all major holidays
- [ ] **More hours** — Set for departments if applicable (kitchen, pharmacy, etc.)

---

## Business Description

- [ ] Written and published (don't leave blank)
- [ ] Under 750 characters
- [ ] Includes primary services/products
- [ ] Includes primary service area/location
- [ ] Natural keyword inclusion (not stuffed)
- [ ] Focuses on what makes the business unique
- [ ] Includes a call-to-action
- [ ] No promotional language or URLs (against guidelines)

### Template
```
[Business Name] is a [primary category] serving [service area] since [year].
We specialize in [primary service 1], [primary service 2], and [primary service 3].
[Unique value proposition]. [Call-to-action or differentiator].
```

---

## Products & Services

- [ ] All primary services listed
- [ ] Each service has a description
- [ ] Prices included where applicable
- [ ] Links to relevant website pages
- [ ] Products listed with photos and prices (if retail/restaurant)
- [ ] Menu items added (restaurants)
- [ ] Service categories organized logically

---

## Photos & Visual Content

### Required photos
- [ ] Logo (square, clear at small sizes)
- [ ] Cover photo (1080x608px recommended)
- [ ] Exterior photo (helps customers find you)
- [ ] Interior photos (at least 3)
- [ ] Team photos (builds trust)
- [ ] Product/service photos (show what you do)

### Photo best practices
- [ ] Minimum 10 total photos
- [ ] Photos are high quality (well-lit, in-focus)
- [ ] No stock photos (Google may detect and remove)
- [ ] Geo-tagged if possible (adds location signal)
- [ ] Updated regularly (add new photos monthly)
- [ ] File names descriptive: `austin-plumber-pipe-repair.jpg`

---

## Reviews

- [ ] Review count: 10+ minimum, 50+ preferred
- [ ] Average rating: 4.0+ stars
- [ ] Recent reviews (within last 30 days)
- [ ] All reviews have owner responses
- [ ] Negative reviews addressed professionally
- [ ] Review generation process in place
- [ ] Direct review link distributed to customers

---

## Posts

- [ ] Posting at least weekly
- [ ] Each post has a photo/image
- [ ] Each post has a CTA button
- [ ] Mix of post types (Updates, Offers, Events)
- [ ] Posts include relevant keywords naturally
- [ ] Offers have clear start/end dates
- [ ] Events have accurate date/time/location

---

## Q&A

- [ ] Common questions pre-seeded by business
- [ ] All customer questions answered
- [ ] Answers include relevant keywords naturally
- [ ] Responses are timely (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Inaccurate answers reported/corrected

---

## Attributes

- [ ] All relevant attributes completed
- [ ] Accessibility attributes set
- [ ] Payment methods listed
- [ ] Amenities listed (Wi-Fi, parking, etc.)
- [ ] Health & safety attributes (if applicable)
- [ ] LGBTQ+ friendly, women-owned, etc. (if applicable)

---

## Verification & Maintenance

- [ ] Profile is verified (blue checkmark)
- [ ] No duplicate profiles for the same location
- [ ] Monitoring for suggested edits (Google users can suggest changes)
- [ ] Monthly review of all fields for accuracy
- [ ] Competitor profiles monitored for category/feature changes
- [ ] GBP Insights reviewed monthly (views, searches, actions)

---

## Common GBP Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| Adding keywords to business name | Profile suspension |
| Using a virtual office address | Profile suspension |
| Having multiple profiles for same location | Confusion, split reviews |
| Not responding to reviews | Missed ranking signal |
| Using stock photos | May be removed by Google |
| Outdated hours | Negative reviews, lost customers |
| Wrong category | Lower rankings for target searches |
| No posts for months | Signals inactive business |
| Ignoring suggested edits | Incorrect info may be published |
